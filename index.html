Bear Trap 101

Below, you will find everything you need to know to succeed and earn the most points during bear trap. We will go over how to run a perfect rally, join rallies the right way, and basically how the whole thing runs.

When to Call Your Rally

First, let’s discuss calling a rally. Each player will be assigned to a quadrant. You will call a new rally every 5ish minutes (depending on your march time).

So each quadrant will call its first rallies as follows:

Front row 30:00 min
Quadrant A 29:30 min
Quadrant B 29:00 min
Quadrant C 28:30 min
Quadrant D 28:00 min

The times listed are those left of bear trap so you can use the timer below the trap as a guide to when you should call your rally. As soon as your rally returns home, start another one, so you stay within your quadrant.

Now you may ask, why does it matter when I call my rally? To put it in simple terms, we want to space out our rallies to avoid waiting 5 minutes for our marches to return. This will allow us to join way more rallies.

The first set of rallies will take 5 minutes to return, but once your marches come back, you would join the top rally, which would be leaving a lot earlier than the rally you just came back from.

Calling Your Own Rally

Use your strongest heroes for your own rallies.

Recommended heroes are Mia, Gwen, Hector/Jeronimo.

Instructions on how to set up and save your troop formations are below.

Your top 3 heroes will default into the formation slots.

Note: Hector has a special skill that grants Marksman 10% - 50% additional damage. He’s a great hero to invest in for arena and bear trap lead. You can get his shards from the Lucky Wheel.

Next, we want to change our troop ratio. Tap on Balance.

Your troop ratio should be: 1% / 29% / 70%

Note: If you are using the Snow Ape skill to increase your march size for the rallies you lead, update the balanced troops after activating pet skills.

Make sure to press Save and save it in slot 1.

Joining Rallies the Right Way

So we will be implementing a few new rules to give everyone the best chance at getting a good score.

First, let's review our hero selection. We currently have 6 heroes you can choose from to lead your rallies. The hero’s are Reina, Jessie, Jasser, Seo-yoon, Jeronimo and Gwen.

The big question is, why these heroes? Let me show you.

When you view a rally, you can see all rally stats and bonuses by tapping on the little orange flag button.

What we are interested in, for bear trap damage, are the Hero Skills. As a Rally Captain, your top hero’s Expedition Skills are the skills being triggered during the attack. (Exploration Skills are used in the arena).

Each rally gets an additional four (4) Expedition Skills from joining players. These skills are selected by who joins the rally first, to whose leading hero has the most stars.

If your hero is selected to be a rally bonus, the skill must be either additional damage or attack. The bonus skill is the first Expedition Skill of your lead hero.

Above you can see that Jessie adds 25% more damage to all troops. On the right, you can see that this is Jessie’s first Expedition Skill. All other skills don’t matter at this point.

This is why it’s so important for everyone to select the correct hero leads. If you send Charlie (with a level 5 first expedition skill) as your lead, he will grab a buff slot, and cannot be replaced. The rally will be pretty good at mining coal, but no better at damaging the bear. Everyone in that rally will do 25% less damage than they would have, had Charlie not crashed the party.
